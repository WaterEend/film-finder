The Website

Film Finder was originally planned to be a website where you could search up movies and shows and see where to watch them, if they were restricted to specific countries and aditional useful infromation. I got the idea from always having trouble figuring out where to watch movies I wanted to see and decided it was a good idea to see how far I would come. Of course I didn't expect to fully finish this website for this project but I did hope to see how much was possible and in the end it's still in a very very early stage, I noticed throughout the coding process it was a lot more complicated than I expected so at the moment the site is heavily limited and can only do a few things.

For in the future I might try to slowly continue working on this website as I get better at coding but this of course will take some time and it won't be close to working well anytime soon. That doesn't mean you shouldn't take a look around the website, you might find yourself a new show you might like or find a movie you want to rewatch.
